Construct a network from its model file.

Callers may specify which ArmNN parser to use. The input/output tensor names must be specified in order to obtain the appropriate ::armnn::BindingPointInfo objects. In the future, it may be necessary to accept tensor shapes as well, e.g. to support armnnTfParser.
